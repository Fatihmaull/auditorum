How It Works

Three Steps to Unbreakable Trust

Reports stay off-chain. Only a cryptographic fingerprint is anchored to Solana — making verification instant, public, and tamper-proof.

1

Anchor

The auditor uploads the finalized report. Auditorum computes its SHA-256 hash and stores it on Solana with metadata — immutably and forever.

2

Share

The enterprise receives a Proof ID and an embeddable verification badge. They share the report with stakeholders alongside the proof.

3

Verify

Anyone uploads the report to our public portal. Auditorum re-hashes it and compares against the on-chain proof. Instant verification — no account needed.
